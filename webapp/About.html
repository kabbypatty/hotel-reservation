Hilton

More than 60 years after Conrad Hilton founded the first ever Hilton Hotel in Texas, his son launched Conrad Hotels…and the brand was established in 1982. Conrad was founded with the goal of operating an elite network of luxury hotels and resorts in the world’s major destinations. The vision…to be the luxury hotel of choice for discerning travellers. Born among traditional luxury hotels and from a need for a brand with connectivity and gracious cultural delivery, Conrad Hotels & Resorts premiered its first property in 1985. It has since grown its collection and now has 23 award-winning hotels and resorts located in global gateway cities and exclusive leisure destinations throughout Asia, Europe, the Middle East and the Americas. Maintaining strong roots in its foundation, Conrad Hotel & Resorts is committed to providing global modern luxury and personalised travel experiences to all its guests, in a high-tech, high-touch environment.
